Itty bitty lets you create tiny sites and apps that are stored in the link itself. To make them, you can edit this text directly, or drag an HTML file in to generate a link.

Use it to share poetry, quick reference, tools, and more.

The content you create here is stored entirely in the link. It isn't even sent to this server, so you have full control of its privacy.

When done, you can share through email, twitter, qr codes, domain redirects and the many other places you can put a link. Access is entirely based on where you choose to put it. Note: Every medium has a maximum size usually ranging from 1000 to 10000 bytes.

Chrome:10,000 bytes
Twitter: 4,088 bytes
Slack:4,000 bytes
QR Code:2,610 bytes
Bitly:2,048 bytes
IE:2,048 bytes
